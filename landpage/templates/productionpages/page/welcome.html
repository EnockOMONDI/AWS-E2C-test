What is LADA?

The Leadership and Development Academy (LADA) is established in Africa as a joint initiative of Springdale College UK now known as ForMission College, accredited by York St John University and Newman University in Birmingham, Africa for SDGs and ICY AFRICA which runs the Springdale Foundation and other global partners.



What are LADA COURSES?

This are Diploma programmes, designed to expose junior African leaders to the complexities of Leadership, Governance and development issues facing the African continent. Once you update your student profile you can browse courses and enroll in a course of your choice.



What are LADA TRAINING BITS?

Training bits are small tools or small learning pieces, derived from the courses. Each training bit has something for you to learn at your own pace. Once you update your student profile you can browse Training bits and enroll in a Training bit of your choice.
